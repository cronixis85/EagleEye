Product description

Zinus has reinvented the bed frame with this "compack" and easy-to-set-up base, uniquely designed for optimum support and durability. This bed fame adjusts to provide a sturdy foundation from a full size up to a king size mattress set. Visionary Design - The center bar support and 9-leg construction is unique to the Compack Bed Frame and ensures superior stability. Low-Stress, Easy Assembly - The Zinus Compack bed frame assembles in a few simple steps. The 9 support legs are intelligently recessed for safety and helps you avoid hitting your toes when making the bed. Bed frames are meant to be used with a box spring and mattress set. Worry free 5 year limited warranty.

3.0 out of 5 starsWatch out for BAD SPOT WELDS!
BySHAWMANon February 15, 2016
Verified Purchase
It's a decent adjustable bed frame, as long as you receive one that doesn't have a broken weld. Seems to be very sturdy and intelligently designed. It was very easy to put together, however the first frame I ordered had a bad spot weld. Amazon was great in sending out a new frame, but it was very inconvenient to tear down my Select Comfort air mattress, disassemble the bad frame, reassemble the new one and put everything back together. Not to mention the new frame had a bent rail that needed straightening. Also, good luck fitting a defective frame back into the original box without tearing up the cardboard. Make sure you check all the spot welds before assembly!
