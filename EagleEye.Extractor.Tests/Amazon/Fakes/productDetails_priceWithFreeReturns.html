Compression Socks for Men and Women - Perfect for Running, Sports, Flight Travel, Pregnancy and Daily Wear

What Our Customers Say

Wearing These Socks During My Travels Keep My Feet Unswollen

I love these socks! While I don't have major blood circulation issues, my feet and ankles tend to swell if I'm sitting in a car or on a plane for hours on end. Wearing these socks during my travels, my feet and ankles are not getting swollen. The socks are not itchy and work well in cool or warm temperatures. Highly recommended.

Worked Really Well On My 10 Mile Run Through the Mud and Woods

I wore these socks for a 10 mile run through the mud and woods, and I have to say that these socks worked really well. Very comfortable! They stayed up during the entire run while I was pulling myself over obstacles. They have protected me from multiple cuts on my legs as everyone else on my team got their legs pretty torn up.

Help to Reduce Fatigue, Swelling and Improve Recovery

aZengear compression socks are stylish and comfortable, at the same time featuring anti-bacterial, anti-slip, breathable and sweat-absorbent fabric.

Made from some of the best materials combining nylon and spandex, our compression socks help to reduce fatigue and swelling in your lower leg as well as provide relief for varicose veins. These socks help to improve blood circulation and reduce muscle fatigue if you spend a long time on your feet. The socks also help to regulate the temperature as well as promote faster muscle recovery.

Created to Last Without Compromising Comfort

Double stitched heel design with anti-bacterial fabric allows to feel the immediate comfort while having a good style. The socks also wash well without losing compression.

The socks come in a very attractive packaging that is suitable for gifting and are available in 2 colors (blue and pink) as well as multiple sizes.

Carefully designed and manufactured to provide premium support, comfort and relief while boosting your mobility and performance.
